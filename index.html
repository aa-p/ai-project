Welcome to the AI Project

Exploring Pinterest analytics with responsible, read-only AI.

About This Project

The AI Project helps users understand Pinterest trends and content performance in a transparent way. We focus on providing analytics and insights by only using “GET” requests (read-only data retrieval) from the Pinterest API.

Our goal is to help creators and businesses discover top-performing topics and better plan their content strategy. However, we do not:

  • Write or modify user content on Pinterest (no publishing, editing, or deleting Pins).
  • Store personal user data without explicit consent.
  • Perform automated actions like saving, following, commenting, or sending messages.
  • Scrape or extract data in any way that violates Pinterest’s terms or community guidelines.
  • Sell or share Pinterest user data with third parties.
  • Offer competitor research or benchmarking without explicit Pinterest authorization.

Acceptable Use & Compliance

Our read-only integration aligns with Pinterest’s Developer Guidelines by focusing on:

  • Content Marketing & Measurement Tools: We analyze publicly available Pins to provide trend insights and performance metrics, strictly via GET endpoints.
  • Creative Tools: We may offer AI-driven suggestions to creators based on popular content, but do not automatically create or schedule Pins.
  • Advertiser & Audience Insights (Within Policy): We respect user privacy and do not store or combine Pinterest data with external data for targeting or monetization.

We do not perform any user actions on Pinterest. The application only sends read-only (GET) requests to retrieve data, ensuring end users remain in full control of their Pinterest accounts and actions.

Privacy & Data Handling

We handle any Pinterest data in strict compliance with our Privacy Policy. If you grant our AI Project limited access to your Pinterest data via OAuth, we only use that temporary token for retrieving (GET) relevant information that you have authorized. We never write, delete, or alter Pinterest content on your behalf.

Our project is not intended for children under 13. If you have questions about our data practices, please see our Privacy Policy.

Our Mission

We aim to discover trends on Pinterest using responsible, policy-compliant methods. By focusing on read-only analytics, we empower users with data-driven insights without ever automating or performing any unauthorized actions on their accounts.

Users can rely on our analysis to shape their content strategy, while remaining confident that their Pinterest experience is fully within their control and compliant with Pinterest guidelines.
