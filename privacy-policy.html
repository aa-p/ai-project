Privacy Policy for Trend Tracker

Effective Date: March 31, 2025

Welcome to Trend Tracker (“we,” “us,” or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our website or services (collectively, the “Service”). Our Service involves analyzing publicly available Pinterest content (“Pins”) to predict or illustrate trending topics and insights.

By using Trend Tracker, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our Service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Pinterest OAuth Data

If you choose to connect your Pinterest account, we may receive basic account data and an access token via Pinterest’s OAuth process. This data may include your public profile information and any additional information you explicitly authorize us to access.

We do not store or permanently save any data from your Pinterest account (apart from temporary tokens necessary for making API requests to deliver our Service). We only retrieve data from Pinterest as needed in real time.

1.2 Automatically Collected Data

Log Data: Our hosting environment (e.g., GitHub Pages) may automatically collect information such as your IP address, browser type, referring/exit pages, and timestamps. We use this information only for basic analytics and to ensure the Service is functioning correctly.

Cookies: We do not directly set cookies on this site for user tracking. However, third-party services (like Pinterest, iframes, or embedded content) may use cookies. Please review their respective privacy policies.

1.3 User Communications

If you contact us (for example, via email or a contact form), we may keep a record of that communication and any personal information you provide, solely for responding to your inquiry.

2. How We Use Your Information

  1. Service Provision: We use OAuth tokens and any requested data from Pinterest only to provide the core functionality of Trend Tracker—namely, analyzing or displaying Pins to show trends or insights.
  2. Trend Predictions: Our Service processes public Pins to predict or detect trends. We credit all Pins to their rightful owners on Pinterest; we do not claim ownership of any of the content pulled from Pinterest.
  3. Compliance with Pinterest Policies: We follow Pinterest’s Developer Guidelines and do not store, share, or sell personally identifiable information obtained through the Pinterest API.
  4. Site Improvements: We may analyze aggregated usage data (e.g., how many people visit a certain page) to improve our Service.

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or otherwise share your personal data with third parties, except as necessary to run the Service (e.g., facilitating communication with Pinterest through its API). We do not provide or sell any information from our API calls to advertisers or other entities outside of Pinterest’s authorized usage.

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Credit and Intellectual Property

  • Pinterest Content: All Pins, images, and trademarks remain the property of their respective owners. We do not claim any ownership over Pinterest’s intellectual property or the Pins we analyze. We merely display or analyze publicly accessible information to provide insights and trending data.
  • Attribution: Where feasible, we link Pins back to their original source on Pinterest (e.g., https://www.pinterest.com/pin/...), and we do not alter or obscure Pinterest content, in compliance with Pinterest’s policies.

5. Data Retention & Security

  • Data Retention: We only maintain temporary access tokens and do not permanently store personal data from Pinterest in our databases. Any logs or analytical data are typically kept in aggregate form for a limited period and then deleted or anonymized.
  • Security Measures: We take reasonable measures to protect against unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no online service can fully eliminate security risks. Please use Trend Tracker at your own discretion.
